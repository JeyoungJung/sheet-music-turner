Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 5, 2026

Sheet Music Turner is an iPad app for reading and annotating sheet music. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

Sheet Music Turner does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account or login is required.
  • No analytics, tracking, or telemetry is used.
  • No data is sent to any server.
  • No third-party SDKs or advertising frameworks are included.

Local Data

All data — imported scores, annotations, setlists — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export or share it using standard iOS sharing features.

File Access

The app requests access to files only when you choose to import a score. It does not access files in the background or without your action.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, you can reach us via the app's App Store listing or at the GitHub repository.
